The Midnight Studio: Capturing the Last Breath of the Soul

In the landscape of 2024-2026 global media, the ENA original series The Midnight Studio (Yahan Sajingwan) stands as a definitive exploration of life, death, and the unresolved emotions that linger in between. Starring Joo Won as Seo Ki-joo, the 7th photographer of a supernatural studio, and Kwon Nara as Han Bom, a struggling lawyer who unexpectedly becomes his "Safe Zone."
